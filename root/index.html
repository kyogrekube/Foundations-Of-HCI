About The Circle's Edge

Mission Statement

Our mission at The Circles Edge, Inc. is to address issues surrounding bulling and how individuals are affected. We strive to foster a sense of belonging for those who find themselves “on the outside” through an array of educational, social, and art oriented programs in an effort to assist individuals who may feel unconnected and isolated. Therefore, The Circle’s Edge, inc., offers meetings, socials, and educational events as vehicle for individuals to get involved and have a sense of belonging.

  • • 501(c)(3) Non-Profit Status
  • • Focus on Bullying Prevention & Support
  • • Serving Capital District (NY) and Mid-Atlantic (DMV)
